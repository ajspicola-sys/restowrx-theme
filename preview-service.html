What is Botox?

Botox and Dysport are FDA-approved injectable neurotoxins that temporarily relax targeted facial muscles to smooth dynamic wrinkles — the lines that form from years of expressions like smiling, squinting, and frowning.

At Livia Med Spa, our expert injectors use advanced micro-dosing techniques to deliver results that look natural and refreshed — never frozen or overdone.

What to Expect

During Your Visit

Your appointment begins with a personalized consultation where your injector will assess your facial anatomy and discuss your goals. The injection process takes just 10-15 minutes using ultra-fine needles — most clients describe it as a tiny pinch.

Recovery & Results

There is virtually no downtime. You may experience slight redness at injection sites that resolves within an hour. Avoid lying down for 4 hours and strenuous exercise for 24 hours. Full results appear within 7-14 days.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does Botox hurt? +
Most clients describe the sensation as a tiny pinch. We use ultra-fine needles and can apply topical numbing cream for comfort.
How long do results last? +
Results typically last 3-4 months. With regular treatments, many clients find their results last longer over time.
What's the difference between Botox and Dysport? +
Both are neurotoxins that work similarly. Dysport tends to spread slightly more, making it ideal for larger areas like the forehead. Your injector will recommend the best option for you.
Can I combine Botox with other treatments? +
Absolutely! Botox pairs beautifully with dermal fillers, chemical peels, and microneedling for comprehensive facial rejuvenation.
